Generated by Claude  ·  Last updated May 2026  ·  Owner: Product Team  ·  Status: Under Review

Objective

Ship the core reviewer experience: structured feedback, AI-powered summarization, and expiring share links. This quarter focuses on closing the feedback loop — getting artifacts in front of the right people and making the resulting conversations actionable without a project management tool.

Success Criteria for Q2

  • Reviewers can leave structured feedback at the deployed URL without creating an account
  • AI summary produces a meaningful digest from 4+ feedback items in under 5 seconds
  • Share links enforce expiry: expired tokens return 410, valid tokens bypass visibility checks
  • All 7 MCP tools verified against deployed URL in Claude Desktop
  • Gallery loads in <1s on a cold Vercel edge request with 3 seeded artifacts
